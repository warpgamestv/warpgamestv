Phloem Logistics
 is the foundational item transport system in Cambium, inspired by the vascular tissue found in real-world plants.

Phloem Duct

The Phloem Duct

acts as a specialized pipe block. Unlike traditional rigid pipes, Phloem Ducts are designed to form a biological plumbing system, bridging the gaps between your machines and chests.

  • Connection: Place Phloem Ducts between containers or machines. It automatically seeks out compatible connections.
  • Limitations: The duct relies on gravity or active pushing/pulling mechanics.

Gravitropic Node

The Gravitropic Node

is an item collection mechanic based on gravitropism.

  • When placed, it scans an 11x11 block radius around itself in search of loose items on the ground.
  • Loose items in range are gently pulled toward the Node. Look at them hovering towards it!
  • Once the item gets close enough, it gets sucked into the Node's internal inventory.
  • You can connect Phloem Ducts to the Gravitropic Node
     to immediately move the acquired items into a centralized storage system!
