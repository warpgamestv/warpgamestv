Resource Trees
 are the primary method of passive resource generation in Cambium. By cultivating these specialized organisms, you can endlessly farm valuable minerals like Iron, Gold, and Diamonds without ever picking up a pickaxe.

Progression

1. Mineral Soil
 Foundation

Resource Saplings are highly specialized and will simply fail to grow on standard dirt or grass. They require Mineral Soil to take root. You must place your sapling directly on top of a Mineral Soil

block.

2. The Root Block (The Brain)

When a Resource Sapling grows, it doesn't just spawn a static structure. Instead, the sapling transforms into a Root Block. The Root Block acts as the "brain" of the tree and sits right above the Mineral Soil

.

  • The Root Block calculates the tree's structure and grows it dynamically over time in a satisfying animation.
  • Trees grow into Living Logs, custom Leaves, and eventually bear Resource Fruits (such as Iron Fruit, Gold Fruit, etc.).

3. Maturation and Harvesting

Once the tree matures, the Resource Fruits will begin to ripen.

  • The fruits have multiple growth stages.
  • Fully mature fruits can be right-clicked to harvest the resource they yield (or they may drop the resource depending on the tree type).
  • Over time, the Root Block will naturally regrow harvested fruits, expending energy from the underlying Mineral Soil
    !
