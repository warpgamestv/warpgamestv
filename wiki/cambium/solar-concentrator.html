The Solar Concentrator

is a high-heat processing machine that utilizes focused sunlight to smelt ores and materials rapidly. Unlike traditional furnaces that burn fuel, the Concentrator runs entirely on solar energy, making it a sustainable but time-dependent solution for material processing.

Obtaining

The Solar Concentrator is crafted using Biopolymer Casing, with a Solar Lens to focus the light.

Crafting Recipe:
  • Top: `Air` | `Solar Lens` | `Air`
  • Middle: `Biopolymer Casing` | `Furnace` | `Biopolymer Casing`
  • Bottom: `Biopolymer Casing` | `Biopolymer Casing` | `Biopolymer Casing`

Note: The Biopolymer Casing must be crafted first using Copper Ingots and Biopolymer

.

Usage

Placement Requirements

For the Solar Concentrator

to function, it must have direct access to the sky.

  • It must be daytime (approx. 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM in-game).
  • The block directly above the machine must be transparent (Air or Glass).
  • It will not function during Rain or Thunderstorms.

Interface (GUI)

The machine interface consists of three main slots:

  1. Input Slot (Left): Place smeltable items here (Ores, Sand, Cobblestone, etc.).
  2. Lens Slot (Bottom Left): Place a Solar Lens
     here to upgrade efficiency.
  3. Output Slot (Right): The processed result appears here.

Mechanics & Upgrades

Processing Speed

The Solar Concentrator

is significantly faster than a standard Furnace, but its reliance on the sun creates a "burst" production cycle.

State Speed Multiplier Cook Time
Standard 1x 72 Ticks (~3.6 seconds)
With Solar Lens
 2x (Turbo) 36 Ticks (~1.8 seconds)
Vanilla Furnace 0.3x 200 Ticks (10 seconds)

The Lens Upgrade

Inserting a Solar Lens

into the upgrade slot doubles the processing speed. This allows the machine to smelt roughly stack of items every 2 minutes during peak sunlight hours.
