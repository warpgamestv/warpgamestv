Xylem Logistics
 is the fluid transport system in Cambium, inspired by the water-conducting tissue of plants. It serves as the fluid equivalent to the item-based Phloem Logistics, focusing primarily on the management of Living Sap.

Living Sap

Living Sap is a vital biological fluid within the Cambium ecosystem. It is naturally produced by Resource Trees
 and can be harvested for advanced crafting and machine processing.

Arboreal Extractor

The Arboreal Extractor

is a specialized machine designed to safely tap into the metabolic network of a Resource Tree
.

  • Placement: Place the Extractor facing a Living Log that is part of an active, mature Resource Tree
    .
  • Operation: Over time, it will passively extract Living Sap from the tree into its internal buffer.
  • Extraction: You can extract the sap from the machine using a Bucket or, more efficiently, an automated duct system.

Xylem Duct

The Xylem Duct

is a specialized biological pipe designed purely for fluid transport.

  • Connection: Place Xylem Ducts between fluid-handling blocks (like the Arboreal Extractor
    ) and storage tanks.
  • Logic: Once connected, the ducts form a cohesive network, intelligently pulling Living Sap from Extractors and pushing it towards available tanks or machines.

Fluid Tank

The Fluid Tank provides essential bulk storage for your Living Sap.

  • It seamlessly integrates with the Fabric Transfer API, meaning it works perfectly with Xylem Ducts and standard Buckets.
  • Right-clicking the tank with a bucket of Living Sap will deposit the fluid, and clicking with an empty bucket will withdraw it.
