The Grafting Tool

is the essential, multi-purpose implement for any dedicated botanist in the Cambium mod. It serves dual purposes: propagating advanced botanical structures and configuring your biological networks.

Crafting

The Grafting Tool

is a durable item, crafted with two Sticks and an Iron Ingot.

Usage

1. Grafting Saplings

To create a complex Resource Tree

, you cannot simply place an ingot into the dirt. You must graft the desired resource into a young sapling.

  • Requirement: An ordinary sapling (like Oak) must be placed in the world.
  • Method: Hold the target resource (e.g., Raw Iron, Iron Ingot, or a tag-supported item like `c:raw_materials/iron`) in your Offhand.
  • Action: Right-click the placed sapling with the Grafting Tool
     in your Main Hand.
  • Outcome: There is a 75% chance of success.
* Success: The sapling will transform into the corresponding Resource Sapling (e.g., Iron Sapling), emitting green particles.

* Failure: The sapling will wither and die, leaving behind a Dead Bush, and emitting smoke particles.

  • Cost: Each attempt consumes one item from your offhand and slightly damages the Grafting Tool
    .

2. Biological Wrenching

The Grafting Tool also acts as a wrench to configure the physical connections of your logistical fluid and item networks.

  • Duct Configuration: Right-click directly onto a Phloem Duct or Xylem Duct to toggle its connection cycle (`NORMAL` -> `EXTRACT` -> `NONE`). You can also right-click the block adjacent to the duct to adjust the duct's connection face touching that block.
  • Strand Configuration: Right-click a Mycelial Strand
     to toggle its behavior between `CONNECTED` and `LOCKED`.
