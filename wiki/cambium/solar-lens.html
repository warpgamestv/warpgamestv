Solar Lens

On This Page

  • Usage

The Solar Lens

is a specialized optic device manufactured to concentrate sunlight into an extreme focal point. It serves as the primary upgrade component for the Solar Concentrator.

Usage

  • Upgrading Machines: The Solar Lens
     can be placed into the dedicated Lens Upgrade Slot located in the bottom-left of the Solar Concentrator
    's interface.
  • Effect: Inserting the Solar Lens
     instantly doubles the processing speed (Turbo Mode) of the Solar Concentrator
    , reducing the minimum cook time from 72 ticks to 36 ticks per operation.
  • Sustainability: The Solar Lens
     is not consumed during operation; it is a permanent structural enhancement to the machine as long as it remains in the slot.
