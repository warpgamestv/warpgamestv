Photovoltaic Armor
 is an endgame suit of protective gear that harnesses the power of the sun to sustain the wearer.

Crafting

The armor pieces are crafted using standard armor patterns. To craft them, you will likely need specialized components from the mod (consult JEI for precise recipes).

Mechanics

Sunlight Self-Repair

While the wearer is exposed to direct daytime sunlight (no blocks overhead and light level > 10), each Photovoltaic Armor

piece will slowly repair its own durability over time.

The "Photosynthesis" Set Bonus

When the player equips a full set of Photovoltaic Armor

(Helmet, Chestplate, Leggings, and Boots) and stands in direct sunlight, they activate the Photosynthesis set bonus:

  • Regeneration: The armor periodically applies the Regeneration effect to the player.
  • Hunger Restoration: The armor will naturally restore the player's hunger over time, provided the armor pieces are not damaged.
