Overview

The Solar Digester

is the heart of the Cambium automation loop. It breaks down complex organic blocks into Organic Ash
.

Usage

Placement

  • Must have a clear view of the sky (transparent blocks like glass are allowed).
  • Stops working during rain or night.

Inputs

  • Valid Items: Logs, Planks, Saplings, Leaves.
  • Catalysts: Focusing Lenses (Optional).

Outputs

  • Byproduct: Organic Ash (15% chance per operation).
