Living Wood
 is the primary timber resource generated by the Resource Trees
. Unlike standard, inert Minecraft wood, Living Wood
 retains a resilient, biological quality perfectly adapted to the Cambium ecosystem.

Components

The Living Wood

set features a complete suite of standard wooden building blocks, all sharing a distinct, organic aesthetic:

  • Living Log: The foundational block that makes up the trunk of mature Resource Trees
    .
  • Living Planks: Crafted from Living Logs, serving as a bright, pristine building material.
  • Living Stairs & Slabs: Standard architectural blocks crafted from Living Planks.
  • Living Fences & Gates: For securing your biological enclosures.
  • Living Door & Trapdoor: Sturdy portals that fit the Solarpunk aesthetic.
  • Living Pressure Plate & Button: Functional redstone components made from the same resilient timber.

Obtaining

Living Logs are passively generated as the structural framework when a Resource Tree grows from its Root Block. You can manually chop them down or incorporate them into your forestry automation.
