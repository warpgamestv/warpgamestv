Velvet Wood

On This Page

  • Velvet Log
  • Velvet Planks
  • Aesthetic Use
Velvet Wood is the native wood type found in the Vesper Grove and Velvet Forest. It features a deep purple bark and a rich, dark violet interior.

Velvet Log

The primary block harvested from Velvet Trees. It is a striking purple color and serves as the foundation for the mod's building palette.

  • Stripping: Velvet Logs can be stripped with an axe to reveal a smoother, lighter purple texture.
  • Wood Block: Can be crafted into Velvet Wood (logs with bark on all 6 sides).

Velvet Planks

Crafted directly from Velvet Logs. They are highly sought after by builders for their vibrant yet dark purple tone.

  • Crafting Recipes: Used to craft the full set of Vesper Wilds wood products:
* Velvet Stairs & Slabs

* Velvet Fences & Gates * Velvet Doors & Trapdoors * Velvet Buttons & Pressure Plates

Aesthetic Use

Velvet Wood pairs excellently with Vesper Stone

and Bioluminescent Moss for a complete "magical forest" aesthetic.
