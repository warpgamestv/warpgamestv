Foraging

On This Page

  • Forageable Items
  • Rewards
Foraging is a primary survival mechanic in Vesper Wilds, encouraging players to explore and interact with the flora rather than just mining.

Forageable Items

  • Glint Berries
    :     Found on bushes or in leaves.
  • Ember Shelf Fungus
    :     Grows on the sides of Velvet Logs in the Velvet Forest.
  • Vesper Sprouts
    :     Small purplish plants found on the forest floor.

Rewards

Frequent foraging allows players to sustain themselves in the Wilds without needing traditional farming (Wheat/Cows).

  • Organic Matter: Foraged items can often be composted quickly.
  • Exotic Ingredients: (Planned) Some foraged plants will be used in advanced alchemy or potion brewing.
