Velvet Leaves

On This Page

  • Ambient Particles
  • Drops
  • Harvesting
Velvet Leaves
 are the foliage of the Velvet Trees. They are deep purple and possess unique ambient properties.

Ambient Particles

The most notable feature of Velvet Leaves

is their interaction with the wind. They periodically release Leaf Particles that drift through the air, creating a serene, living atmosphere in the Vesper Grove.

Drops

  • Stick
    :     Standard drop when decaying or broken.
  • Velvet Sapling: Rare drop used to replant Velvet Trees.
  • Velvet Berry: Occasionally drops a sweet purple berry that can be eaten for quick nourishment.

Harvesting

  • Shears: Use shears to harvest the leaf blocks themselves for decoration.
  • Hoe: A hoe is the fastest tool for clearing large amounts of Velvet Leaves
    .
