Vesper Stone

On This Page

  • Finding
  • Stonecutting & Crafting
  • Durability
Vesper Stone
 is a decorative stone type found exclusively within the Vesper Wilds biomes. It has a dark, slightly indigo tint and a unique "weathered" texture.

Finding

  • Surface Outcroppings: Often found as patches on the surface of the Vesper Grove.
  • Underground: Replaces large sections of standard Stone and Deepslate in the Vesper biomes.

Stonecutting & Crafting

Vesper Stone
 can be processed into several decorative variants:
  • Vesper Stone
     Bricks:     A clean, structural brick variant.
  • Bricked Variants: Stairs, Slabs, and Walls are available for Vesper Stone
     Bricks.
  • Polished Vesper Stone
    :     (Planned) A smoother, more refined version of the stone.

Durability

Vesper Stone
 has similar blast resistance to standard Stone, making it a reliable choice for building safe structures in the Wilds.
