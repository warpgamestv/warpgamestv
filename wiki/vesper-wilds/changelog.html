Version 0.0.31

  • Changes & Bug Fixes:
* Changed the Velvet Fuzz
 texture.

* Changed the Velvet Fern

texture to be not as bright of pink. * Adjusted the size of Glint Berry
 Bush patches in the Vesper Grove biome. * Glint Berry
 Bushes will now randomize in growth states in their berry patches. * Turned down the amount of falling leaves from the Velvet Leaf blocks. * Brand new worlds should not start with a Velvet Eclipse anymore.

Version 0.0.30

  • IMPORTANT: NEW DEPENDENCY! Starting with 0.0.24, Vesper Wilds requires GeckoLib 5.4.2 or higher to run properly.
  • New Features & Content:
* New Hostile Mob: The Gloom Stalker
! A flying, bat-like predator that spawns exclusively within the Vesper Grove.

* New Mechanic: Twilight Resonance! In light levels below 7, mining speed is increased by 20% and attack damage is increased by 2. * New Event: Velvet Eclipse! A dangerous atmospheric event where Gloom Stalkers spawn outside the Vesper Grove, crop growth halts, and Twilight Resonance is enhanced. * New Items: Added the Velvet Cloak

and Velvet Fuzz
. * Spawn Eggs: Added for Velvet Moth
 and Gloom Stalker
.

  • Changes & Tweaks:
* Velvet Moth
 Overhaul: Drops Velvet Fuzz
 instead of string.

* Creative Menu: Reorganized for better navigation.

  • Bug Fixes:
* Fixed Vesper Wells and Block Tags for Velvet Wood
.

* Fixed recipes and blast furnace compatibility for Raw Vesperite

. * Fixed Velvet Fern
 drops and Vesperite Tool tags.

Version 0.0.24-1

  • Changes:
* Updated Raw Vesperite
 to be included in the `#c:raw_materials/vesperite` tag (used for testing with the mod Cambium).

Version 0.0.24

  • New Features & Content:
* Initial early alpha for the Gloom Stalker
 mob.

* Added Vesperite Ores, Shards, Ingots, and a full Vesperite Tool/Armor set. * Added Velvet Fuzz

and Velvet Fern
 blocks.

Version 0.0.19 & 0.0.20

  • Added:
* Vesperite Lantern: Utility block preventing hostile mob spawns in a 16-block radius.

* Vesperite Tools: Full toolset (Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel, Sword, Hoe), slightly faster than Iron. * New Vegetation: Ember Shelf Fungus

, Vesper Sprouts
, and Velvet Fern
. * Vesper Stone
 Family: Exclusive stone type replaces Tuff in the Vesper Grove. * Wood Family completion: Added Velvet Signs, Hanging Signs, Boats, Pressure Plates, and Buttons.

  • Changed:
* Adjusted Vesper Grove grass color and fixed Leaf physics (slower drift).

* Removed Vanilla Stones (Granite, Diorite, Andesite) from Vesper Grove for "cleaner underground development."

Version 0.0.17

  • Added: Custom falling leaf texture for Velvet Leaves
     (previously used cherry leaves).

Version 0.0.15

  • Added: Textures for Velvet Door
     and Velvet Trapdoor
    .
  • Changes: Backend updates for recipe, tag, and loot table handling.

Version 0.0.10

  • Initial Release:
* Initial Plank family (minus some textures).

* Vesper Grove biome and Vesperite Ore. * Custom Trees.
