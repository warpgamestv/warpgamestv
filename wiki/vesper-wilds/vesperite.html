Vesperite

On This Page

  • Twilight Resonance
  • Crafting

Vesperite is the signature mineral of the Vesper Wilds. It is a deep indigo crystal that resonates with the energy of the twilight.

Obtaining

  • Vesperite Ore: Can be found at standard Diamond/Iron depths within Vesper biomes, and occasionally in the Deepslate layer elsewhere.
  • Processing: Smelt Raw Vesperite
     in a furnace to get Vesperite Ingots.

Twilight Resonance

Vesperite tools and weapons possess a unique property called Twilight Resonance. The items become more effective when used in dark or dimly lit environments.

  • Tools: +20% Mining Speed in low light.
  • Weapons: +2 Attack Damage in low light.
  • Applies to: Sword, Pickaxe, Axe, Shovel, and Hoe.
