Glint Berries
 are a bioluminescent food source found growing in the Vesper Wilds.

Obtaining

  • Harvesting: Found on bushes within the Vesper Grove. Right-click a mature bush to harvest the berries without breaking the plant.
  • Drops: Also has a chance to drop when breaking Velvet Leaves
    .

Effects

  • Saturation: Provides a decent amount of hunger and saturation.
  • Glow: (Planned) Eating a Glint Berry
     may grant a short burst of the Glowing effect to the player.

Crafting

  • Sweet Berries: Can be used interchangeably with standard Sweet Berries in some recipes.
  • Bait: Used to attract the Velvet Moth
    .
