Velvet Wood
 is the signature timber of the Vesper Grove. It is prized for its deep purple hue and magical origin.

Harvesting

Velvet Wood
 is obtained from Velvet Trees. Harvesting the logs is the first step for any aspiring builder in the Vesper Grove.

Velvet Log

The raw logs harvested from Velvet Trees.

  • Stripping: Can be stripped with an axe to reveal a smooth, lavender-colored wood.
  • Velvet Wood
    :     Craft 3 logs together to get the "all-bark" variant.

Velvet Planks

The primary building material derived from Velvet Logs.

The Velvet Family

The wood can be crafted into a full suite of building blocks.

Stairs & Slabs

Perfect for creating roofs or detailed flooring.

Fences & Gates

Secure your Vesper Grove retreat.

Doors & Trapdoors

Iconic purple entryways with unique patterns.

Utility

Buttons and pressure plates for your redstone needs.

Bioluminescent Properties

While the wood itself doesn't glow, it is often found in close proximity to Bioluminescent Moss, making it a perfect backdrop for magical builds.
