Velvet Cloak

On This Page

  • Obtaining
  • Special Ability: Slow Falling
  • Visuals

The Velvet Cloak

is a specialized piece of equipment that grants the user magical locomotion.

Obtaining

  • Crafting: Crafted using 8 Velvet Fuzz
     (dropped by the Velvet Moth
    ) in a standard chestplate pattern.
  • Tier: It is considered a lightweight armor piece, offering moderate protection.

Special Ability: Slow Falling

While equipped in the chestplate slot, the Velvet Cloak

grants the player the Slow Falling status effect indefinitely.

  • No Fall Damage: The slow descent prevents all fall damage.
  • Aerial Control: Allows for greater control when jumping off high cliffs in the Vesper Grove.

Visuals

The cloak has a custom Geckolib 3D model that drapes realistically over the player's character.
